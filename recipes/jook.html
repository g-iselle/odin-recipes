Jook (Rice Porridge)

Ingredients

  • 1/2 Cup Uncooked White Rice
  • 5 Cups Water
  • Salt & White Pepper, to taste (optional)
  • Pinch of Sugar (optional)

Steps

  1. Wash the rice in cold water by rubbing the rice between your hands several times, & then draining the cloudy water. Pour the washed rice & 5 cups fresh water into a medium pot & bring it to a boil.
  2. Turn the heat to low & simmer for about an hour, stirring occasionally, until the rice has broken up & the liquid is thick & milky. You can add more water if you'd like a thinner porridge.
  3. Stir in salt & white pepper to taste, or sweeten it with a little sugar (optional). Leave plain or sprinkle on toppings of choice.
