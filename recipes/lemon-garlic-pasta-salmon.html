Lemon-Garlic Pasta with Salmon

Yummy convenient method to transform salmon into a quick, weeknight-friendly dinner!

Are you unsure about how to make the most of your leftover salmon? Here's a tasty and convenient method to transform it into a quick, weeknight-friendly dinner. Remember to set aside a portion of pasta water as it acts as a natural thickener for the lemon-garlic pasta sauce, giving it a velvety texture.

Ingredients

  • 8 ounces whole-wheat pasta
  • 5 tablespoons extra-virgin olive oil
  • 5 cloves garlic, chopped
  • 1 teaspoon anchovy paste
  • ¼ teaspoon crushed red pepper
  • Zest and juice of 1 lemon
  • 1 1/2 cups flaked cooked salmon (see Associated Recipes)
  • 3 tablespoons chopped fresh parsley
  • ¼ teaspoon salt
  • 2 tablespoons whole-wheat breadcrumbs, toasted

Steps

  1. Cook pasta according to package directions. Drain, reserving 1/2 cup cooking water.
  2. Combine oil, garlic, anchovy paste, crushed red pepper, lemon zest and lemon juice in a large skillet. Heat over medium-high heat until sizzling, about 3 minutes.
  3. Add the reserved water, the pasta, salmon, parsley and salt. Cook, stirring, until the sauce coats the pasta, about 2 minutes.
  4. Serve topped with breadcrumbs.
